Our Vision

One of our biggest visions for tea-licious was to create a boba shop no one has ever seen. We wanted to break down the classic stereotypes and preconceived notion of what a boba shop is and should be. We wanted to create a hub for creativity and atmosphere to transport you away from the boba shops you have been used to in the past.
